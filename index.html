About Me

Hi, my name is Shelby, and I am a student at the UCSD extensions Coding Bootcamp. I live in San Diego, California in a house with my dogs and my family. My interests outside of computer programming and web development are art, yoga, dogs, music, skateboarding, and graphic novels. In the beginning of Covid-19; my girlfriend and I had an idea to watch all of the Marvel movies in chronological order. It was one of the best experiences of my life. It was all brand new to me so the journey was incredible. The order was very important. Marvel did such a fantastic job weaving so many story lines together in such an epic way.

This is the order that I watched them in: Captain America: The First Avenger, Captain Marvel, Iron Man, Iron Man 2, The Incredible Hulk, Thor, The Avengers, Iron Man 3, Thor: Dark World, Captain America: Winter Soldier, Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Ant-Man , Captain America: Civil War, Spider-Man: Homecoming, Doctor Strange, Black Panther, Thor: Ragnarok, Avengers: Infinity War, Ant-Man and The Wasp, Avengers: Endgame, and Spider-Man: Far From Home.
